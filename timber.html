What makes our plywood so special

Our team is focused on producing the highest quality product in market. You can always trust Will-Vee Timbers Zimbabwe. Let us help you build your strong houses and good furniture that will last generations to come. The prominent use of Will-Vee Timbers'plywood is in structural applications. Our Plywood can naturally withstand a lot of stress and full weather exposure. The structural plywood we manufacture is best used for beams and hoadings but can also be used in crates, bins, internal structures, outdoor furniture and wood boxes. It offers all the inherent advantages of the parent wood plus enhanced properties in it's laminated structure. It has high impact of resistance Plywood provides high strength to weight ratio and has chemical resistance.

We manufacture good quality shutterply. Our Shutterply C+C is used in construction for shuttering (forming of wet concrete), hoarding of building sites, roof sheathing etc. Our plywood-shutterply is flexible, workable and in-expensive. The quality of our plywood is highly guaranteed.

Our plywood is locally manufactured which makes it in-expensive. Will-Vee Timbers takes extra care when producing plywood because we value our customers and we value our brand. Our product is eco-friendly and at the same time ensures product excellence.
